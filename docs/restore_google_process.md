# Restoring to Google Compute Engine


To restore workloads to Google Compute Engine, use the Restore to Google Compute Engine wizard.

1. [Check prerequisites](restore_google_byb.md).
2. [Launch the Restore to Google Compute Engine wizard](restore_google_launch.md).
3. [Select a workload and restore point](restore_google_virtual_machines.md).
4. [Specify credentials and datacenter settings](restore_google_account.md).
5. [Specify a name](restore_google_name.md).
6. [Specify an instance type](restore_google_instance_type.md).
7. [Select a Google VPC](restore_google_network.md).
8. [Specify secure restore settings](restore_google_secure_restore.md).
9. [Configure a helper appliance](restore_google_proxy_appliance.md).
10. [Specify a restore reason](restore_google_reason.md).
11. [Start the restore process](restore_google_summary.md).
