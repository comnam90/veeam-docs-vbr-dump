In this article

To provide subtenants with individual storage quotas on the cloud repository, you must register a subtenant account for each subtenant. Typically, the procedure of subtenant accounts registration is performed by the tenant on the tenant Veeam backup server. The SP can also manage subtenant accounts for the specific tenant. To learn more, see [Managing Subtenant Accounts on SP Side](cloud_connect_subtenants_manage.md).

You can perform the following operations with subtenant accounts:

* [Add a subtenant account for a standalone tenant account](cloud_connect_subtenant_create.md).
* [Add a subtenant account for a VMware Cloud Director tenant account](subtenant_vcd_create.md).
* [Edit a subtenant account](cloud_connect_subtenants_edit.md).
* [Remove a subtenant account](cloud_connect_subtenants_remove.md).
