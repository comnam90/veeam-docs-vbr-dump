# Creating Protection Group for Individual Computers

In this article

Before you create a protection group for individual computers, [check prerequisites](protection_group_begin.md). Then use the New Protection Group wizard to configure a protection group.

1. [Launch the New Protection Group wizard](protection_group_launch.md).
2. [Specify protection group name and description](protection_group_name.md).
3. [Specify protection scope for the protection group](protection_group_scope.md).
4. [Specify discovery and deployment options](protection_group_options.md).
5. [Specify advanced protection group settings](protection_group_advanced.md).
6. [Review components](protection_group_review.md).
7. [Assess results](protection_group_apply.md).
8. [Finish working with the wizard](protection_group_finish.md).
