# Instant Recovery of Point-in-Time State

In this article

To perform instant recovery of multiple Oracle databases to a selected point-in-time state, perform the following steps:

1. [Launch the Instant Recovery wizard](veor_ir_multiple_pit_wizard.md).
2. [Specify a restore point](veor_ir_multiple_pit_specify_restore_point.md).
3. [Specify switchover settings](veor_ir_multiple_pit_specify_switchover_settings.md).
4. [Finalize the Instant Recovery session](veor_ir_multiple_pit_finalize_ir_session.md).
