# Instant Recovery of Point-in-Time State


To perform instant recovery of multiple databases to a selected point-in-time state, use the Instant Recovery wizard.

1. [Launch the Instant Recovery wizard](vesql_ir_multiple_pit_launch.md).
2. [Select databases you want to recover](vesql_ir_multiple_pit_select_databases.md).
3. [Specify a restore point](vesql_ir_multiple_pit_restore_point.md).
4. [Specify switchover settings](vesql_ir_multiple_pit_switchover.md).
5. [Finalize the Instant Recovery session](vesql_ir_multiple_pit_finish.md).
