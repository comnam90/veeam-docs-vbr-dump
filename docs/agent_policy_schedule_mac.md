# Step 12. Specify Backup Schedule


At the Schedule step of the wizard, specify the schedule according to which you want to perform backup. Backup job scheduling options differ depending on the job mode that you have selected at the [Job Mode](agent_policy_protection_mac.md) step of the wizard:

* [Scheduling Settings for Workstations](agent_policy_schedule_workstation_mac.md)
* [Scheduling Settings for Servers](agent_policy_schedule_server_mac.md)
