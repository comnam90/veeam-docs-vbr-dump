In this article

To instantly recover a VM, use the Instant Recovery to VMware vSphere wizard.

1. [Launch the Instant Recovery wizard](instant_recovery_vsphere_launch.md).
2. [Select a restore point](instant_recovery_vsphere_restorepoints.md).
3. [Select a recovery mode](instant_recovery_vsphere_restoremode.md).
4. [Specify destination settings for the recovered VM](instant_recovery_vsphere_destination.md).
5. [Specify target datastore](instant_recovery_vsphere_datastore.md).
6. [Review the recovery settings](instant_recovery_vsphere_summary.md).
