In this article

To instantly recover a VM, use the Instant Recovery to Microsoft Hyper-V wizard.

1. [Launch the Instant Recovery wizard](instant_recovery_hv_launch.md).
2. [Select a restore point](instant_recovery_hv_restorepoints.md).
3. [Select a recovery mode](instant_recovery_hv_restoremode.md).
4. [Specify destination settings for the recovered VM](instant_recovery_hv_destination.md).
5. [Specify a target datastore](instant_recovery_hv_datastore.md).
6. [Configure network mapping](instant_recovery_hv_network.md).
7. [Review the recovery settings](instant_recovery_hv_summary.md).
