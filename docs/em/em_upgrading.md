In this article

Before you upgrade Veeam Backup Enterprise Manager to version 13.0, [check prerequisites](em_upgrade_byb.md).

To upgrade Veeam Backup Enterprise Manager, take the following steps:

1. [Start the upgrade wizard](em_upgrade_launch_wizard.md).
2. [Select Enterprise Manager as a product to upgrade](em_upgrade_select_product.md).
3. [Read and accept the license agreements](em_upgrade_accept_license_agreement.md).
4. [Provide a license file](em_upgrade_install_license.md).
5. [Install missing software](em_upgrade_system_configuration_check.md).
6. [Review the components that will be upgraded and begin the upgrade process](em_upgrade_review_components.md).
7. [Finalize the upgrade](em_upgrade_finalize.md).
